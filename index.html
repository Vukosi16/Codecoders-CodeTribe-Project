What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence, commonly referred to as AI, has become a ubiquitous term in today's technological landscape. But what exactly is AI? In its simplest form, AI refers to the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding.
